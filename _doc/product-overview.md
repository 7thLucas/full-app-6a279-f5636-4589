# EscapeOps — Product Overview

## What It Is

EscapeOps is an all-in-one operations platform purpose-built for escape room businesses. It centralizes room bookings, puzzle maintenance, and team scheduling into a single dashboard — helping owners maximize booking fill rates and keep puzzle experiences fresh for repeat visitors.

## Target Users

| Persona | Role | Primary Concern |
|---|---|---|
| **Business Owner** | Owns or operates 1–5 escape room venues | Revenue, utilization, repeat visit rate |
| **Room Manager** | Manages day-to-day operations and bookings | Availability, capacity, booking flow |
| **Game Master** | Runs sessions, maintains puzzles, works shifts | Puzzle freshness, shift clarity, post-session notes |

## Core Product Pillars

### 1. Booking Management
- Real-time availability calendar across all rooms
- Group size and capacity controls per room
- Pricing tiers and promo codes
- Waitlist management for fully-booked slots
- Booking analytics and fill-rate reporting

### 2. Puzzle Maintenance
- Puzzle inventory per room with status tracking (active / under repair / retired)
- Maintenance logs and repair history per puzzle
- Freshness score per puzzle based on play frequency and time since last update
- Rotation alerts when puzzles are overdue for refresh or swap
- Post-session notes submitted by game masters after each run

### 3. Team Scheduling
- Drag-and-drop staff scheduling interface
- Game master assignment per room per session
- Shift visibility for all staff roles
- Conflict and gap detection (unassigned sessions surfaced proactively)
- Role-based access: owner / manager / game master

## Strategic Goals

- **Maximize bookings** — fill as many slots as possible via smart availability management, waitlists, and actionable analytics
- **Keep it fresh** — surface when puzzles are getting stale for repeat visitors and prompt rotation or maintenance before experience degrades
- **Reduce admin overhead** — replace spreadsheets and manual coordination with a unified operations tool
